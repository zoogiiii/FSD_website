About Us

Founded 21 years ago, Deeksha Granio Impex has been a trusted name in the natural stone industry, offering premium-quality granite and marble sourced from our quarries located across India’s rich geological landscape.

Our mining and quarrying operations are strategically situated in the heart of India’s finest stone-producing regions, including:

  • Rajasthan: Known for its exquisite marble varieties and sandstone.
  • Karnataka: Famous for its stunning black granite and versatile stone options.
  • Andhra Pradesh: Renowned for unique granites with intricate textures.
  • Tamil Nadu: Home to exceptional marble and high-grade granite.

Over the years, we’ve built a legacy of delivering elegance, durability, and unmatched quality. Our materials are meticulously extracted, processed, and crafted to meet the diverse needs of architects, designers, and homeowners, whether for grand interiors, modern exteriors, or bespoke artistic creations.

At Deeksha Granio Impex, we are committed to sustainability, precision, and customer satisfaction, ensuring every slab we deliver embodies the rich heritage of India’s natural resources and our passion for excellence.

Join us on this journey of crafting timeless beauty for your spaces.
